Welcome

Metaphysics Research is dedicated to advancing philosophical inquiry through the application of advanced artificial intelligence. Our mission is to probe the fundamental nature of reality by leveraging AI's unique perspective, free from human biases, to evaluate metaphysical frameworks.

Our inaugural study, conducted in April 2025, utilized 16 cutting-edge AI models to assess frameworks like analytic idealism, neutral monism, and physicalism. The findings, detailed in our paper, suggest a provocative convergence toward non-physicalist paradigms. Explore the raw data and methodology at our Data page, or learn more about the researcher behind this work at About.

Featured Study

Evaluating Metaphysical Frameworks Through Advanced AI: A Study of Convergence in April 2025
This pioneering experiment reveals AI's preference for analytic idealism (49%) and neutral monism (39%) over physicalism (0%), challenging academic orthodoxy. Read the full paper at metaphysicsresearch.org/paper/metaphysics_ai_202504.pdf or access the dataset and responses at metaphysicsresearch.org/data202504/list.html.
